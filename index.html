ABOUT US

Roxbury Preparatory Charter School is a public school that prepares students to enter, succeed in, and graduate from college. Here at Roxbury, students are given constant reminders of what they can become - anything they set their minds to. students are immersed in an engaging teaching environment where teachers receive real-time feedback on their teaching style and make it their top priority to know and love each student stepping into their classroom.
